notion无法上传文件（已解决）

2021-10-07

Contents

  • 问题描述
  • 解决
  • 笔记

问题描述

I can’t upload images in the web version of notion (windows). i’ve tried chrome and edge, and i’ve tried turning off all browser plugins, nothing works. i can upload images normally from another computer.

解决

I’ve figured out what was going on and everything is fine now. It’s because my internet is provided by my university and some sites were blocked, including s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com, where the notion file seems to be? So I couldn’t upload the images properly before. I emailed my university to ask them to allow access to this website and now there is no more problem.
